Appearing within an architectural décor surrounded by hinterland views and garden blooms in the Grand Palais Éphémère in Paris, ambassadors and friends of the House Sébastien Tellier, Anna Mouglalis, Charlotte Casiraghi, Ramata-Toulaye Sy and Riley Keough share their impressions on the CHANEL Spring-Summer 2024 Ready-to-Wear show.
The collection is instilled with a summerlong, artistic spirit inspired by the villa Noailles, in the south of France, and the joyful, creative and leisurely art de vivre it stands for.

Directed by Caroline de Maigret.
