The creative energy of the north of England inspired Virginie Viard for the CHANEL 2023/24 Métiers d’art collection. Shown on Thomas Street in Manchester, it features glamorous expressions of British style across the decades, like a sky blue guipure lace minidress with a scooped décolleté, its high-waisted velvet skirt embroidered with black sequins and cinched with a multi-buckle chain belt dotted with double Cs.
