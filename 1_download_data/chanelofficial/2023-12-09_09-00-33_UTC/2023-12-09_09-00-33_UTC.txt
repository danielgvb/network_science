Thinking of the CHANEL tweed suit with the ease of a t-shirt, Virginie Viard imagined slim, fitted jackets with open collars for the CHANEL 2023/24 Métiers d’art collection shown on Thomas Street in Manchester. Here, a dark grey metallic tweed skirt suit with lion’s head buttons is playfully embellished with multicoloured double C badges on low-slung hip pockets, and worn with flat, smooth leather Mary-Jane’s strapped with CHANEL’s charms.
