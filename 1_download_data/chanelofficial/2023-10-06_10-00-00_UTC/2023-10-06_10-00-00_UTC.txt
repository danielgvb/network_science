Riffing on the dream of the Little Black Dress, Virginie Viard revealed a suite of sheer silhouettes for evening for the Spring-Summer 2024 Ready-to-Wear collection. Crafted in ruffled tiers of tulle, lace and organza embroidered with crystal plastrons, lace hems and stripes of white ribbon, they evoke a certain idea of sensuality and transparency for endless layering.
