Reflecting the colours of the landscapes of England, a silhouette from the CHANEL 2023/24 Métiers d’art collection by Virginie Viard shown on Thomas Street in Manchester features a mottled patchwork effect in cosy layers of Shetland wool knitwear, starting from a mock neck zippered sweater worn beneath a short knitted jacket with grey tortoiseshell-effect buttons belted like a robe.
