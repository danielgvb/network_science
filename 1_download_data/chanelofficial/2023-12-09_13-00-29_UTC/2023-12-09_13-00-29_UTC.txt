Envisaging new characters with a vibrant, pop spirit, the CHANEL 2023/24 Métiers d’art collection by Virginie Viard shown on Thomas Street in Manchester is infused with subtle nods to British style. Here, a tweed shirt with a polo collar and a trompe l’oeil pinafore front embroidered with white sequin camellias is worn with a matching newsboy cap and a midnight blue chequered tweed miniskirt with low hip pockets and a crochet trim.
