𝘔𝘢𝘯𝘤𝘩𝘦𝘴𝘵𝘦𝘳𝘮𝘰𝘥𝘦𝘳𝘯: 𝘱𝘢𝘴𝘵 𝘱𝘳𝘦𝘴𝘦𝘯𝘵 𝘧𝘶𝘵𝘶𝘳𝘦 – a three-day exhibition, was curated by Factory International and 𝘊𝘏𝘈𝘖𝘚 𝘚𝘪𝘹𝘵𝘺𝘕𝘪𝘯𝘦 with the support of CHANEL and ran from December 8th to 10th at the Victoria Baths in Manchester following the CHANEL 2023/24 Métiers d’arts show. 

The exhibition, open to all, gave a unique perspective on the tremendous history and creativity of Manchester by bringing together artists in the fields of photography, music, poetry, design, crafts, in resonance with the exceptional savoir-faire of the Maisons d’art at 𝘭𝘦19M. It also enabled the discovery of the art of embroidery during participative workshops animated by artisans and experts from Lemarié, Lesage and Atelier Montex.
