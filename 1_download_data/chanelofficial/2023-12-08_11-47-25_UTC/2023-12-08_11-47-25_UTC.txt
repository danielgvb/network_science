Gathering diverse expressions of CHANEL tweed, the CHANEL 2023/24 Métiers d’art collection, shown on Thomas Street in Manchester, began with a suite of coat dresses and skirt suits in piquant shades of raspberry, apricot or apple green. 
Exploring silhouettes nipped close to the body, Virginie Viard twists the notion of the CHANEL suit with short yet demure proportions, adorning them with crochet or smocked braids and contrast jacquard silk linings that extend to collars and cuffs.
