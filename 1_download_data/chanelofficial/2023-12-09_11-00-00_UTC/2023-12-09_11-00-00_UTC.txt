Inspired by Gabrielle Chanel’s art of tailoring, Virginie Viard reimagines CHANEL tweed in youthful cuts for the CHANEL 2023/24 Métiers d’art collection shown in Manchester, like a double-breasted overcoat in a midnight blue chequered tweed flecked with silver threads and softened by strings of pearls encrusted with bows, double Cs and heart-shaped charms.
