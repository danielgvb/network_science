With an air of elegance, joy, and leisure, the CHANEL Spring-Summer 2024 Ready-to-Wear collection evokes the creative life of the villa Noailles in the south of France. Invited to the show held at the Grand Palais Éphémère, ambassadors and friends of the House, Camila Morrone, Penélope Cruz, Brie Larson and Amandla Stenberg, shared their impressions about the sophistication and casualness that run through the collection, the influence of sportswear and artistic refinement, in an architectural décor inspired by the flowers and views of Hyères.

Directed by Caroline de Maigret.
