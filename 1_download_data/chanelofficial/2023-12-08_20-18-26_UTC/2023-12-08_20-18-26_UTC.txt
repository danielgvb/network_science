CHANEL in Manchester – the 2023/24 Métiers d’art show is imbued with the energy of Manchester and its musicality. Through silhouettes of absolute precision, the collection evokes the ever-youthful love story between the House and Great Britain, an adventure that has lasted for over a century.

See more at chanel.com

Music supervision by Michel Gaubert.
Set design by Willo Perron.
