Travelling north to Manchester for the CHANEL 2023/24 Métiers d’art collection, Virginie Viard reflected on Gabrielle Chanel’s love of Great Britain, inflecting CHANEL’s tweeds with a British flair. 
Here, a chequered winter tweed coat-dress in pink and raspberry shades is cut with a small spread collar and bracelet sleeves, worn with a shorter matching miniskirt, a newsboy cap, and the classic CHANEL bag in ombré quilted leather.
