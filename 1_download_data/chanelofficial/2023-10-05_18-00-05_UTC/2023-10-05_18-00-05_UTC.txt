Inspired by the gardens of the villa Noailles, the setting of the CHANEL Spring-Summer 2024 Ready-to-Wear show transports guests to the south of France. Ambassadors and friends of the House Emilia Clarke, Wang Yibo, Rebecca Marder, JENNIE and Paris Hilton talk about the collection in this cubist-inspired space, where bigger than life flowers and large windows invite to contemplation and creative expression.

Directed by Caroline de Maigret.
