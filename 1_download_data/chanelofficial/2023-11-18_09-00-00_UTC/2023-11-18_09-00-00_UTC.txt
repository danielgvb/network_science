The eleventh edition of the Rendez-vous littéraires rue Cambon [Literary Rendezvous at Rue Cambon] turns the spotlight on Maria Pourchet. On this occasion, the writer and screenwriter reflects on the way writing came to her at a very young age "as the very idea of the future". As she considers the act of writing as a necessity, she encourages aspiring writers to take the plunge whatever the cost.
